Programming Club

Description: We believe the tech field and computer science are becoming more and more saturated as many individuals see a job field with flexible hours, the possibility for remote work, and a high salary, and assume tech to be the best option. However, what many students don't account for or do not have the knowledge of is that the computer science major is focused more on theory or understanding why computers work the way they do rather than building the practical skills that will be necessary to secure a high paying job in the tech field. As this field grows the need for tech literate positions and skilled software engineers also rises. That is why our mission in creating this club is to help build these practical skills that members will be able to use in the future to secure a job. This club will include technical skills such as practical Web development basics such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React along with a sprinkle of backend and interview prep through programs such as Leetcode and FreeCodeCamp. Along with building soft skills such as cooperation, leadership, and an innovative thought process. The club will center its curriculum around small group work of 5 people or less to build connections between the students and prepare them for a career where cooperative work is necessary. Finally, we want to provide opportunities for networking whether it be with tech companies, or colleges that focus deeper into tech. Finding the right connections to push towards a successful future is our main objective.
