Anoka-Ramsey Coding Club (ARCC)

The official club to help students become better programmers, network, and providing resources to students at Anoka-Ramsey Community College.

Campus Independent

Resources

We provide resources to aid you with your journey along the computer science pathway, such as information on how to get free courses and services.

Networking

In order to help you succeed in the land of computer science, ARCC provides an excellent opportunity to network with others on the CS path.

Skills

Not only do we provide resources to sharpen your skills in CS, we (and our members) are resources to hone your skills like specific languages, backend development, and frontend development.
